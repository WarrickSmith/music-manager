### Key Notes:  
1. **Program Types:**  
   - **Short Program (SP)** and **Free Skate (FS)** are required for **Basic Novice** through **Senior** grades in Singles.  
   - Lower grades (Kiwi Skate to Juvenile) and Adult/Masters events typically have a single program (e.g., Free Skate only).  

2. **Gender Splits:**  
   - Applied to skaters aged 12+ in Pre-Elementary to Juvenile.  
   - Mandatory for Basic Novice to Senior.  

3. **Discipline-Specific Rules:**  
   - Pairs/Ice Dance/Synchro: No gender splits or program splits beyond inherent team composition.  
   - Showcase: Optional splits at organizers’ discretion.  

This list aligns with NZIFSA guidelines and ensures compliance with national competition standards. Let me know if further refinements are needed!
